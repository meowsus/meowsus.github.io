What I Use

When I was six years old, my father bought a Mac SE. He gave me the great honor of pressing the power button first and from the moment I watched the watched the introductory Hypercard demo I was building weird little stacks for my Dad and leaving them on his desktop for him to find later.

Flash forward eight years, one day after school at a friend's house, my life changed forever. I into his Dad's office, expecting to play Descent, but instead found my friend furiously typing in Notepad. He would clickity-klack for a while and then tab over to Internet Explorer and refresh the browser. I asked what he was doing, but he shushed me, so I just sat and watched him build what was what turned out to be a website. For the next three months I learned enough HTML through osmosis and HtmlGoodies.com to build my own website.
